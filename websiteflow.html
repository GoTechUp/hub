Website Flow

The following table outlines the structure of GoTechUp's website, including different pages and sections that provide a seamless user experience, alongside its integration with the WooCommerce platform for online shopping.

Page Description Connection to WooCommerce
Home The homepage introduces GoTechUp's mission and services. Displays featured products and integrates with WooCommerce for direct purchase links.
About Us Information about GoTechUp, our history, and our goals. Provides a link to shop and explore products related to our services.
Shop Our online store featuring products and educational tools. Fully integrated with WooCommerce for browsing and purchasing.
Contact A contact page to get in touch with customer support and inquiries. Contains a link to WooCommerce support for customer service and order issues.
